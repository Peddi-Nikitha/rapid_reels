## Quick Download Instructions

### Option 1: Manual Download
1. Visit the links above
2. Search for images matching the descriptions
3. Download high-quality images (at least 1080x1920)
4. Rename and save to: `rapid_reels_app/assets/images/`
   - `onboarding_birthday.jpg`
   - `onboarding_car.jpg`
   - `onboarding_influencer.jpg`
